# Chakh-na-Brand-Project
Chakh-Na – Branding Strategy & IMC Campaign | Marketing Innovation Project
Chakh-Na is a comprehensive Marketing Innovation (MI) project focused on building a healthy snack brand from scratch and designing a complete Branding Strategy + Integrated Marketing Communication (IMC) Campaign.
The project covers end-to-end brand creation—from market research to launch execution and performance tracking.

Project Overview

This project develops a strategic blueprint for Chakh-Na, a modern healthy-snack brand offering Amla Candy, Coconut Barfi, Makhana, Sundal, Thattai, and Gazzak.
It aims to address a clear consumer gap: healthy snacks that don’t compromise on taste.

Key Components of the Project
1. Market Research & Consumer Insights
Identified unmet need for tasty but healthy snacks.
Survey-based insights on taste preference, convenience, and willingness to pay.
SWOT, PESTEL, Porter’s Analysis for competitive understanding.
Target audience profiling (18-45 yrs, fitness-oriented, urban, health-conscious).

2. Product & Brand Development
Defined Value Proposition: Healthy + Tasty + Convenient.
USP: “Deliciously Healthy Snacks for Every Occasion”
Brand personality: vibrant, trustworthy, health-conscious.
Packaging, pricing, and design concepts for each SKU.

3. Branding Strategy
Brand Name: Chakh-Na
Tagline: “Healthy Bites, Happy Hearts”
Visual identity in green & orange representing health and vitality.
Consistent messaging built on psychology of health, authenticity & taste.

4. Integrated Marketing Communication (IMC)
Includes a fully structured IMC plan covering:
Digital marketing (social media, SEO, influencer collabs)
Traditional media (print ads, billboards)
Launch-phase strategy (pre-launch teasers, engagement, promotions)
Detailed budget allocation and timeline

5. Engagement Strategy
Social media contests, health-based challenges, polls, and recipes.
Influencers + community events + PR outreach.
Website concept for e-commerce and brand storytelling.

6. Performance Tracking & KPIs
Sales volume, CAC, CLV, engagement metrics.
Sentiment analysis through reviews & social listening.
Continuous improvement & scalability plan with new product lines and subscription models.
